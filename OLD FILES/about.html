About EnStock

GET TO KNOW US.

Founded in 2023, EnStock is a streetwear brand that is engaged in the design and sales of apparel and sneakers. EnStock is an American company founded by Bronx Puerto Rican designer and artist Evania Torres. EnStock's name derives from Torres' bilingual background, with EnStock's translation meaning 'In Stock'. EnStock's main mission is to develop, design, and inspire everyone through apparel and streetwear that will make them feel unstoppable.
